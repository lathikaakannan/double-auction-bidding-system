Empowering Farmers, Serving Customers

Agriculture is the primary mainstay of the economy in our country. In recent years because of uncertain trends in climate and other fluctuations in the price trends, the price of the crop has varied to a larger level. Farmers remain oblivious of these uncertainties, which spoils the crops and causes massive loss.

Our AI-powered system uses Machine Learning's Decision Tree Regression Algorithm to predict crop prices accurately. The attributes considered for prediction include rainfall, wholesale price index, month, and year. This gives farmers an advance forecast to maximize their profits and customers the best prices.
